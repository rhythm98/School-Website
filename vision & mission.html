Vision & Mission

 Broadly speaking our aim is to impart all round quality education to meritorious children with a view to shape them into well rounded, well disciplined, well adjusted and well-tuned individuals, capable of making their marks in various walks of life! the academic excellence, character building, inculcation of discipline and leadership training. We are designed to achieve our stated aim by favourable teacher-pupil ratio, efficient prefectural system, residential character and island of excellence in a sea of mediocrity. Combining best of both the Western and the Oriental traditions in education we have put our vision to produce many high-profile soldiers, statesmen, civil servants, administrators, technocrats and captains in the field of business and industry. Our mission is to play an important role in strengthening the silken bonds of national integration and promoting international understanding. Our vision is to become pacesetting institution comparable with the best of our kind in the world.
