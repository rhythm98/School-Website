Our Classrooms


The 'classroom' is the most important space of our school where the child feels comfortable & has a sense of belonging.

Sports


Sports occupy a prominent place in or curriculum.Right attitude, tolerance, self control respect for rules and fair play, working as a team and honesty are cultivated through various physical activities.

Our teachers are the facilitators who guide each child for their overall development and performance. Each child is made to feel special. The development of multiple intelligence is stressed through instruction & performance complenenting each other.
