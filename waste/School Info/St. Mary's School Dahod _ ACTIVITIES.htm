 The focus and objective is to inculcate children to appreciate beauty and help them develop a sense of aesthetics. In addition to sketching and painting, students are exposed to other media including clay and paper Mache etc. The focus is on attracting children into various arts and also NURTURING the growth of the “ARTIST” in every child.

In the present curriculum the importance of lab has increased due to the fact that students cannot learn properly unless they conduct the experiment themselves. Keeping in mind the theory of learning by doing the school has following labs:

  • Physics Lab : The high tech physics lab is well equipped with all the apparatus required for better understanding of the subject and various scientific laws till class XII

  • Chemistry Lab : The high tech chemistry lab is equipped with the latest and high standard equipment and chemicals required upto class XII.

  • Bio Lab : The bio lab contains each and every sample, specimen and equipment required upto class XII.

  • Language Lab : The language lab has been equipped with the latest apparatus for enhancing the speaking skills, listening skills and personality development.

  • Maths Lab : The idea of math lab has been conceptualized to make the children understand the fundamentals of the subject effectively. The apparatus like Abacus, Geo-lard, Water Analogous Model etc. are used for better understanding of various topics of Arithmetic Mensuration, Trigonometry etc.

  • Computer Lab : St. Mary's school has two well equipped computer labs. Computer education is imparted students from class 1 to 12.
