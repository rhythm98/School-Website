Welcome


Welcome to the Example Easy School Website. This site has been created to demonstrate how schools can very easily create and maintain their own website using Easy School Website.

This entire website has been created using the Easy School Website system. The design was created in less than two minutes, and then we started adding our page content using the simple page editor. All of this was done for free within a standard web browser.

It's really easy to add news to your website, so that important announcements are published quickly. If your school has a Twitter account, you can add your feed to your homepage by simply entering yout account name!

Schools are welcome to register and build their website completely free of charge - we only charge your school once you are ready to publish your website live on the internet.

If you are interested in how you can build a similar website for your school that is both easy to create and maintain, please visit us at www.EasySchoolWebsite.com
